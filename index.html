About Us

Who we are?

We are small and private Grand Theft Auto Online Crew who like to play Grand Theft Auto Online for Fun and Passing Missions and Heists.

What are our goals?

Our goals are pretty simple: play and enjoy in playing game in missions, heists and other crew based activites like Selling Businesses and Nighclub missions.

What we do?

In game we are playing Hesits (Original, Doomsday and Casino), Contact Missions (Simeon, Leaster, Ron, Trevor etc.), Selling and Retrieving Business Missions (Bunker, MC Businesses, Air Freight) and Races (Cars, Bikes, Planes, Arena, Open Wheel Series, Stunt Series). Also we enjoy in helping each other in Daily Objectives.
